Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju (born 23 October 1979), known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian actor who works predominantly in Telugu cinema.[4] Referred to in the media as the "Rebel Star", he is one of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema. [5] Prabhas has been featuring in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2015 [6][7][8] and has received seven Filmfare Awards nominations, a Nandi Award, and a SIIMA Award.

Early life and education

Prabhas was born on 23 October 1979 in a Telugu family to film producer Uppalapati Surya Narayana Raju and Siva Kumari in Madras, Tamil Nadu. The youngest of three children, he has an elder brother, Prabodh, and elder sister, Pragathi. [3][17][18] He is the nephew of Telugu film actor Krishnam Raju. [3] His family hails from Mogalthur, near Bhimavaram of West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. [19]
